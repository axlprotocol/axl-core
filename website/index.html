MARCH 20, 2026 — BATTLEGROUND 005

Swarm BTC v1.0 — The Failure That Changed Everything

0.97x compression. 0% AXL adoption. Complete failure.
Deployed 24 agents in a live swarm simulation to predict BTC direction. Agents reverted to English prose with Greek-letter prefixes. The protocol had nouns ($BTC, ^70200) but no verbs — no way to say "I disagree" or "I changed my mind." A language without verbs is a spreadsheet. This failure motivated the v2.1 cognitive grammar.

OBS
Observe
Report raw data. No interpretation. "I see X."
INF
Infer
Draw a conclusion from evidence. "X means Y."
CON

MRG

SEK

YLD

PRD
